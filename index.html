What Is Little Wishes?

Little Wishes is a youth-led initiative that introduces young children to a variety of topics and career fields, including medicine, technology, and more, through interactive and age-appropriate activities.

What We Do

Educational Classes

We teach kids about real-world topics and skills in a fun, engaging way.

Little Wishes Bags

Kids decorate care bags that are donated to hospital patients.

Community Involvement

Students learn the importance of service and giving back.

Featured Programs
