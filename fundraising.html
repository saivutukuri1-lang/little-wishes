Bringing comfort and joy to hospital patients

Our Care Bag Program

Through fundraisers, Little Wishes creates care bags decorated by children and donated to hospital patients to bring comfort and joy.

What's Inside Our Care Bags

  • Hand-decorated cards and artwork from children
  • Comfort items like soft blankets or socks
  • Entertainment items such as coloring books and crayons
  • Personal care essentials
  • Inspirational messages and positive notes
  • Small toys and activities for children patients

How You Can Help

Your donations help us purchase supplies, materials, and items to fill our care bags. Every contribution makes a difference in bringing smiles to hospital patients.
